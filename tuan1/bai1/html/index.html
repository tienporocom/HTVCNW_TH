Consequently. he becomes aequainted with the inner city streets. and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless. and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawver is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm. The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
